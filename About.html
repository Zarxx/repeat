About this webiste


Hello, I am Adrian Jasik I have created this website in order to share my... photography with others
As I created this website i decided to include viewers and give them the ability to upload and
share their own work. At first I just wanted to create a website to archive my work for personal
viewing but after reconsideration I thought it would be a good idea to maybe get others involved
and try to create a small community of photographers. All work is welcome here, be it amateur or
professional. Have fun and enjoy the website.
